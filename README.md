This repository contains an article presenting a simulation study investigating the performance of the modified SCCS for detecting increased risk of SIDS after DTaP vaccine doses.
The repository contains documents for three different articles/threads:

Article 1: https://x.com/compose/articles/edit/2070554948492750848

1. A pdf of the article
2. R scripts used to build the underlying SIDS and vaccination distributions, to randomly generate virtual population data, to fit the modified SCCS, to conduct all simulations, and to generate figures, and reproduce all results in the paper.

Article 2: https://x.com/compose/articles/edit/2071430530743066624
4. A pdf of an article presenting a response to Jikkyleaks' critiques
5. R scripts with Jikkyleaks' simulation study and my additions to perform null simulations and include continuous age at dose distributions to correct the two major errors/oversights in his work

Article 3: x.com thread https://x.com/jsm2334/status/2074322827759497323?s=20
6. Figures of SIDS by age from four published papers, and an overlaid plot of the normalized age distributions for these papers.
7. An .xls file with the extracted monthly distribution levels for each of the four studies.
8. Various papers cited in the thread
9. An unrolled pdf version of the x.com thread has detailed documentation to generate the .xls file and reproduce the age distribution plots
